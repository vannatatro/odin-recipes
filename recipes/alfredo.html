Alfredo

Description

This rich and creamy sauce pairs phenomenally with any type of pasta and best of all, it only takes a couple minutes to make! So skip the storebought jar of alfredo sauce and make this savory, garlicky version that is sure to please a crowd.

This sauce is so versatile. You can use it on pasta, breadsticks, or even as pizza sauce! Garnish with parsley and some fresh cracked black pepper and you are sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 ½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Add butter and garlic to a saucepan over low heat. Sauté for about 1 minute or until garlic is fragrant, being careful not to let it burn.
  2. Stir in cream and simmer until it has thickend slightly, about 5 minutes
  3. Add the grated Parmesan cheeese and mix until completely melted. Then stir in parsley and enjoy!
